# PXC Partner Guide (Demo)

- PXC provides wholesale full fibre for broadband, TV/streaming, mobile backhaul, and business connectivity; partnerships focus on driving broadband-first growth with value-adds (WiFi assurance, multiroom/streaming).  
- Target segments: households in fibre-ready areas, SMEs needing reliable connectivity, and public sector sites requiring uptime SLAs.  
- Offers to emphasize: gigabit and multi-gigabit tiers, unlimited data, partner TV/streaming bundles, SIM-only add-ons, and professional install.  
- Sales motions: digital demand gen, retail/field sales in upgraded footprints, and B2B direct for dedicated internet and Ethernet.  
- Competitive positioning: highlight speed and reliability versus incumbent providers and local altnets; bundle economics to reduce churn.  
- Compliance: accurately represent coverage; confirm address availability before quoting.  
- Demo-only guidance; not an official commercial document.

# PXC CEO Demo Script (Exec Summary)

Show instant answers on fibre build pace, net adds, bundle uptake, churn, and service quality using the PXC Executive Agent.

**Suggested flow**
1) **Footprint & Reliability**: Ask for fibre-ready premises, WiFi Guarantee adoption, and comparative performance versus legacy connections.  
2) **Customer Growth**: Track broadband + TV bundle penetration, SIM cross-sell, and net adds by region (London & South East, Midlands, North West, Scotland, Wales).  
3) **Financials**: Summarise GBP revenue mix across broadband, TV, mobile/backhaul, and business connectivity; highlight capex focus on fibre rollout.  
4) **Customer Experience**: Review NPS, outage drivers, and top actions improving retention.  
5) **Competitive Positioning**: Compare offers versus incumbent providers and regional altnets by speed tier and reliability.  

_Demo-only script using staged PXC data and documents._

# PXC Network Overview (Demo)

- UK-wide wholesale full fibre network delivering symmetric broadband and Ethernet; independent of incumbent operators.  
- Rolling out XGS-PON full fibre with advertised speeds up to 5.5Gb and improved reliability versus legacy FTTC/HFC.  
- Focus regions include London & South East, Midlands, North West, Yorkshire & Humber, Scotland, Wales, and South West cities.  
- WiFi assurance options delivered via partners; mesh WiFi supported for in-home coverage.  
- Resilience: diverse backbone rings, proactive monitoring, and reroute policies to reduce latency and jitter for streaming, gaming, and business apps.  
- Mobile/backhaul: PXC provides small-cell and macro backhaul; traffic handed off at MVNO/interconnect sites (no PXC-owned RAN).
- B2B services include dedicated internet access, Ethernet, dark fibre, and managed connectivity for SMEs, enterprise, and public sector sites.  
- Competitors: incumbent FTTP providers and regional altnets; positioning on wholesale choice, speed, and service quality.  
- Demo content only; performance figures are illustrative.

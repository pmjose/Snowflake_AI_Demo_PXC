# PXC Financial Snapshot (Demo)

- PXC is the UK's fastest growing independent wholesale telecoms platform, providing broadband/ethernet access to ISP partners and business/public sector customers nationwide.  
- Portfolio revenue is diversified across wholesale FTTP, business DIA/Ethernet, dark fibre, small-cell/5G backhaul, and managed services.  
- Full-fibre (XGS-PON) rollout is underway to deliver multi-gigabit (up to 5.5Gb) speeds with stronger reliability and lower churn risk.  
- Marketing investment is aligned to broadband-first bundles, speed upgrades, and partner co-marketing to defend share against incumbent providers and altnets.  
- Capital is focused on fibre rollout, resilience, WiFi assurance, and smart-city backhaul as differentiators.  
- Cash cost focus: network modernization, field efficiency, and vendor/contract optimisation.  
- All financials in pounds sterling (GBP); figures are illustrative and for demo only.
